Websites I've Created

During high school, I have found (and often still do) myself making websites for various clubs. So far, I have helped work on 2 websites for 2 different clubs. One was for the Game and Animation Club (AKA G/A Club), made entirely from scratch, and one was for my school's athletics program (AKA d.athletics), which was made alongside a team of club members.

G/A Website: https://ga-club.glitch.me/index.html | d.athletics Website: https://www.dtechathletics.com/
