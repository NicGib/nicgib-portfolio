Red Latinoamericana de Innovación Frugal (RELIF) is the Latin American branch of the Frugal Innovation Hub at Santa Clara University.

Restructured and currently updating website for the Latin American Branch of SCU's Frugal Innovation Hub; coded in HTML, CSS, PHP, and JavaScript. Developing alongside weekly feedback from FIH director and RELIF members. Implementing requested changes with quick turnaround. All content in Spanish.

RELIF Official Site

Rede Brasileira de Inovação Frugal (REBRIF) is the Brazilian chapter of RELIF.

With feedback from REBRIF members, developed an informational webpage within the RELIF website in order to notify users of an upcoming conference, increasing subscription & attendance rates. All content in Portuguese.
